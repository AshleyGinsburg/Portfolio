Hurricane Progression Map

For a very long time hurricanes have been one of the deadliest natural disasters and North Carolina has experienced many of them from being a coastal state. In this map, I chose highlight three of the deadliest hurricanes to ever hit the coast of North Carolina.

Costa Rica

This map highlights to importance of the country of Costa Rica, which is home to an array of flora and fauna. I have chosen to highlight the tree canopy cover and the rigid terrain of the country.

Tribe Territories in the Grand Canyon

The Grand Canyon is known as a world wonder and a must see for many travelers across the World, which is why I chose to highlight the territories of the tribes who still reside in the area today. By highlighting these tribes I hope that it gives an insight to show the history behind the canyon and show that these lands are sacred lands.
